Our Quality Process

How we preserve nature's biological vitality at every stage of craft.

1. Purified Sourcing

We select only certified 100% organic crop harvests from regenerative California farms. Soil purity and sustainable crop rotation are verified prior to selection.

2. Cold-Wash Sanitization

Produce is triple-washed in 4°C ozone-purified water. This specialized system removes micro-impurities while keeping vitamins and live enzymes intact.

3. Slow-Press Mastication

Fitted with slow hydraulic masticators, we extract elixirs under high pressure without heat. This prevents oxidation and locks in biological vitality.

4. Sterile Cold Chain

Juices are bottled in pharmaceutical-grade sterile glass jars and stored immediately under a strict 2-4°C cold chain until the moment of pickup.
