Our Genesis

Crafting the Million-Dollar Standard

Verde & Vita was founded in 2023 with a singular mission: to eliminate the compromises found in traditional pasteurized juices. We set out to design a clean wellness ritual that replicates the immediate freshness of a farm harvest.

We partnered with organic growers, wellness biochemists, and local baristas to formulate high-end raw juices, wellness shots, and superfood bowls. Each ingredient has a metabolic purpose.
