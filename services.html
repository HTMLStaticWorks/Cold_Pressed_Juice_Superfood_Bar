The Raw Truth

Not all juices are created equal. Here is how our slow-press process compares to supermarket alternatives.

Standard Pasteurization

  • Heat Treated (Pasteurized) Heated up to 160°F to extend shelf life. This heat destroys sensitive raw enzymes and vitamins.
  • Preservatives & Concentrates Often reconstituted from concentrate with artificial colors, added water, sugars, and preservatives.
  • Plastic Bottles Bottled in single-use plastics that leach microplastics and chemical flavor components over time.
  • Weeks on Shelves Juices sit in retail warehouses and shelves for up to 6 months before purchase.
Premium Raw Choice

Verde & Vita Cold-Pressed

  • Zero-Heat Hydraulic Slow-Press Squeezed gently under 15 tons of pressure. Zero friction, zero heat. 100% of vitamins, minerals, and bio-active enzymes remain intact.
  • 100% Pure Organic Ingredients Zero water, sugar, or chemicals. Each bottle contains up to 3 lbs of raw organic green vegetables, roots, and citrus fruits.
  • Eco-Friendly Reusable Glass Bottled in premium medical-grade glass to preserve pure flavor integrity. Sterilized and recycled in our circular loop program.
  • Pressed and Shipped Daily Never stored. Pressed at dawn, hand-packed, and shipped directly to your cooler. Best consumed raw within 4 days.
