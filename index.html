Harvest to Bottle

How We Craft Wellness

Step 01

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with certified California organic farms to source crisp leafy greens, sweet citrus, and fresh ginger roots at peak harvest hour.

Step 02

Hydraulic Cold Pressing

Using custom hydraulic presses, ingredients are compressed under high pressure without heat, saving sensitive minerals, active enzymes, and flavor.

Step 03

Minimal Glass Bottling

Our juices are immediately bottled in reusable pharmaceutical-grade glass jars to preserve taste integrity and reduce plastic microcontamination.

Step 04

Cold Deliveries

We transport bottles in cooled zero-emission trucks straight to our bars or your doorstep, keeping the active nutrients fully alive.
